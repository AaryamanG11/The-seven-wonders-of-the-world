Built by the famous Mughal Emperor, Shah Jahan for his favorite wife, Mumtaz Mahal, the iconic structure of the Taj Mahal, is the pride of India and more importantly, the epitome of love. Built as early as the 16th century, Taj Mahal is the crown of India which is constructed purely of ivory-white marble and is best witnessed during sunrise.





Best Time To Visit:November to March
Location: Dharmapuri, Forest Colony, Tajganj, Agra, Uttar Pradesh
Nearby Attractions: Tomb of I’timad-ud-Daulah, Mehtab Bagh, Jama Mosque, Agra Fort, and more.
Other Things To Do In Agra: Sightseeing in Agra, tour the Khas Mahal, shopping in Kinari Bazaar, visit the Jehangir’s Palace, and more.
